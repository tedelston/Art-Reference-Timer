Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Art Reference Timer ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") accesses, uses, and protects information when you use the application to connect to your Google account and view your Google Photos reference albums.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

2. Information we access

When you sign in with Google and grant the requested permissions, the App may access:

Google account basic information:
Your Google account identifier and email address for authentication.
Google Photos Library data (read-only):
List of albums, metadata for albums containing "Reference" in title, and metadata for photos in selected albums.

The App does not request or use write access to your Google Photos Library.

3. How we use your information

We use this information solely to:

  • Authenticate you with your Google account
  • Display your selected reference albums
  • Show images in timed drawing sessions

We do not use your Photos data for advertising or analytics.

4. Data storage and retention

Data is processed in memory only during your browser session. We do not store, log, or persist your photos on servers.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell or share your Google Photos data with third parties.

6. Your choices

  • Revoke access anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Choose which albums to display
